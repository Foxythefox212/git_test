Honey Garlic Pork Chops

World's Best Honey Garlic Pork Chops

A quick and simple grilled pork chop that everyone will love
featuring a simple and easy glaze.

Ingredients

  • ½ cup ketchup
  • 2⅔ tablespoons honey
  • 2 tablespoons low-sodium soy sauce
  • 2 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 6 (4 ounce) (1-inch thick) pork chops

Directions

  1. Preheat grill for medium heat and lightly oil the grate.
  2. Whisk ketchup, honey, soy sauce, and garlic together in a bowl
    to make a glaze.
  3. Sear the pork chops on both sides on the preheated grill.
    Lightly brush glaze onto each side of the chops as they cook; grill until no longer
    pink in the center, about 7 to 9 minutes per side. An instant-read
    thermometer inserted into the center should read 145 degrees F (63
    degrees C).

When you're in need of a quick and easy dinner that the whole family will
